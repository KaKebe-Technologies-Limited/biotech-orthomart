# **1\. Project Overview**

The Biotech website will be a modern, professional, and responsive healthcare technology platform designed to showcase the company’s orthopedic, rehabilitative, prosthetic, orthotic, and surgical product solutions.

The website will position Biotech as a trusted medical solutions provider serving hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, distributors, government institutions, NGOs, and individual clients.

The platform will emphasize:

* Innovation in orthopedic and rehabilitative healthcare  
* Professional medical product distribution  
* Clinical support services  
* Trust, compliance, and certifications  
* Corporate credibility and partnerships
